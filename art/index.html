Welcome to my Pixel Art Gallery!

One of my goals in life is to be good at both logical and creative forms of thinking. My studies are already sharpening my logical skills (in my opinion too much sharpening), therefore I need to use my creativity in personal projects and hobbies outside of University. That's where Pixel Art comes in to play, in fact ever since I was young I always wanted to be able to draw Pixel Art. I find the art style addictive to look at. Pixel Art also evokes some sort of deep nostalgia in me which probably stems from the fact that video game graphics tended to be more pixel-y when I was young. Therefore I took it upon myself to learn Pixel Art, and most importantly get good at it to an extent where I could develop my own assets if I were to make my own video game one day :) (this I will do as well).
